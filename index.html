The Team

  • Gary Bannatyne

    Gary is driven by doing things that matter. With an extensive background in enterprise development, he has been instrumental in inspiring and shaping the lives of young people and start-up businesses across Africa. His passion lies in using technology for social change.

  • Brandon Muller

    Combining a knack for numbers with a love of technology, Brandon’s passion lies in financial forecasting and management. With a CIMA qualification behind his name, he ensures that corporate partners’ investments are optimally used.

  • James Coetzee

    James is a serial entrepreneur who has founded three sustainable ventures. He is also a business development consultant. His passion lies in adding long-term value to businesses.

  • Melisha Moodley

    Pursuing her passion in training and youth development is Melisha. She has a background in Multimedia Design as well as in Training. Inspired by the success stories of past Interns, she is driven to make an impact in educating the youth together with the Technical team.

  • Matshepo Thusi

    A self-motivated individual who prefers to let her work speak for her! Being in the educational sector for over 8 years she understands the importance of teaching the right skills to get the best opportunities and results. Her people-centric approach and drive make her an excellent team player.

  • Dylan Knevitt

    Dylan has been using computers from the age of 6, and coding from the age of 12. A curious mind, completely obsessed with all things tech. His passion lies in equipping the youth with the skills and knowledge to succeed in the ever evolving tech space.

  • Hardy Lutula

    Hardy is a young passionate individual originally from the DR Congo who enjoys spending his time learning and writing source code on a daily basis. Writing source code has been his best hobby since he met the so-called "Software engineering" individual hiding behind computers.
